Bring your building back to 21st century

The IoT allows your office to be sensed or controlled remotely across existing network infrastructure. Track and analys workplace preformance to gain your office efficiency.

Sensors

The integration of zonifero with motion sensors allows dynamic display of occupancy of rooms and desks on the map. The functionality is especially useful in the case of rapidly changing availability of rooms. The use of motion sensors in the office allows you to generate heat maps for offices.

Real-time conference room and desk availability updates

Zonifero business supports the booking of hot-desks, meeting and other rooms. Reservations can be made from the application on the tablet in front of the room or directly from the calendar in Office365 or G Suite. Room reservations can also be made for businesses that do not use Office 365 or G Suite.
